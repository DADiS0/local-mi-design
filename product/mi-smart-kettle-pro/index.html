      Control your kettle from the appCustomize your keep-warm temperatureThe keep-warm temperature and time can be set in the Mi Home/Xiaomi Home app. The temperature of each mode can be adjusted between 40–90℃. Up to 12 hours of keep-warm time. You can enable "keep warm after pick-up" or "keep warm after boil", based on your user habits*.*"Keep warm after pick-up" and "keep warm after boil" are not automatically enabled in the app. If you enable "keep warm after pick-up" in the app, and remove the kettle from the base whilst the kettle is in keep-warm mode, the kettle will return to keep-warm mode when returned to the base. If you enable "keep warm after boil" in the app, after the water has boiled, the kettle will reduce and maintain the temperature of the water to the temperature setting of the keep-warm mode.
      SCR sound insulationSleep well with no disturbancesWith the addition of the SCR component, when the water temperature falls below the keep-warm temperature, low-power temperature heating is enabled, to keep water warm without any noise. No more being woken up by sudden heating noises while you are asleep.
      From calm to boiling in just 5 minutesThrough the 1800W high-power energy-focusing heating ring in the base, water is thoroughly heated from the bottom to the top. Water comes to a boil in around 5 minutes*.*Data from testing at the VIOMI Laboratory: depending on the environment and actual usage, data will vary.
      Intelligent water temperature controlNo more excess boilingFor mineral water or for reheating water that does not need to be fully boiled, press the "keep-warm" button, water will be heated to and maintained at the temperature you set, so there's no need to repeatedly boil and keep warm.

      The double-layer kettle body design insulates heat, keeping the kettle cool to the touchThe Mi Smart Kettle Pro features a double-layer body with a hollow insulation cavity between the stainless steel liner and the outer housing to protect you from the high temperatures inside the kettle, preventing burns even after boiling.
      Triple power protectionSafety for peace of mindThe Mi Smart Kettle Pro is designed with triple power protection:Firstly, after the water is completely boiled, the power is automatically shut off. Secondly, when dry-burning is detected, the power is automatically shut off. Additionally, the specially designed power connector provides effective protection against electric shocks and creepage.

      13 cm large integrated linerThe spout is created through a one-piece stainless steel stretching technique, allowing it to be seamlessly integrated with the interior. The bottom of the kettle is smooth and has no hard-to-reach spots that impede cleaning. Fitted with a large 13 cm diameter spout for easy cleaning.
      Two-stage damping lid designThe lid is opened using a two-stage damping design. The lid can open to a smaller degree, so that the hot water beads gathered on the lid will not splash out, preventing burning, or to a wider degree, allowing you to fill the kettle and clean the interior with ease.
      High-precision temperature sensorWhen the sensor detects that the water has reached the desired temperature, the kettle will automatically stop boiling or enable the keep-warm program, based upon your settings.
      Power cord storageThe excess length of the power cord can be wound around and stored inside the kettle base for a neater and more elegant look, both when or when not in use.
